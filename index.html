TPI Golf Fitness Assessment

Every golfer’s body moves differently. The TPI Golf Fitness Assessment identifies your range of motion, flexibility, balance, and strength. This data reveals the physical limitations affecting your swing. Once we understand those, we build a program to correct them, improving consistency and reducing risk of injury.

  • Range of Motion: Unlock your body’s rotation for a more powerful swing.
  • Strength: Build core stability for greater accuracy and endurance.
  • Balance: Maintain control throughout your swing motion.
  • Flexibility: Protect joints and generate speed safely.
